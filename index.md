# Welcome to my personal website!

👋 Hi, I’m **Daniele Cannatella**, an Assistant Professor in <a href="https://www.tudelft.nl/en/architecture-and-the-built-environment/about-the-faculty/departments/urbanism" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Urban Data for Design</a> at <a href="https://www.tudelft.nl" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">TU Delft</a>, in the <a href="https://www.tudelft.nl/en/architecture-and-the-built-environment/about-the-faculty/departments/urbanism" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Department of Urbanism</a>.

My work focuses on the intersection of cities, data, and design — exploring how computational tools can help us better understand and shape urban environments. I’m passionate about bridging design thinking with data-driven methods to support more informed, creative, and inclusive urban futures.

I also run <a href="https://rbanism.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Rbanism</a>, a platform where I share research, tools, and thoughts on data-driven urbanism.
