# Welcome to my personal website!

👋 Hi, I’m **Daniele Cannatella**, an Assistant Professor in [Urban Data for Design](https://www.tudelft.nl/en/architecture-and-the-built-environment/about-the-faculty/departments/urbanism) at [TU Delft](https://www.tudelft.nl), in the [Department of Urbanism](https://www.tudelft.nl/en/architecture-and-the-built-environment/about-the-faculty/departments/urbanism).

My work focuses on the intersection of cities, data, and design — exploring how computational tools can help us better understand and shape urban environments. I’m passionate about bridging design thinking with data-driven methods to support more informed, creative, and inclusive urban futures.

I also run [Rbanism](https://rbanism.org), a platform where I share research, tools, and thoughts on data-driven urbanism.
